# Instrumentation Factor Taxonomy
Status: Draft

## Main Factor Groups
- Measurement basis: variable, range, accuracy, response need
- Device selection: sensing principle, materials, installation, environment
- Safety logic: SIL target, proof-test philosophy, fail-safe mode
- Reliability and drift: drift rate, MTBF, calibration history
- Control performance: valve capacity margin, loop stability, signal quality

## Governing Questions
- What drives device selection and range?
- What sets SIL adequacy and proof-test interval?
- Which issues are measurement problems versus process or electrical interference?
