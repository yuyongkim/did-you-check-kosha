# Vessel Interfaces And Validation
Status: Draft

## Cross-Discipline Interfaces
- Piping:
  - nozzle loads, flange ratings, inspection boundary alignment
- Rotating:
  - exchanger or drum interfaces affecting connected machinery
- Instrumentation:
  - nozzle count, level and pressure tapping integrity
- Steel:
  - platform and support load transfer
- Civil:
  - foundation settlement and support movement effects

## Golden Case Families
- Uniform corrosion with remaining-life screening
- Local thinning near nozzle or attachment
- External-pressure concern for tall or thin shell
- Repair or rerating review case with interval impact

## Coverage Goal
- Cover normal thickness checks and edge cases that escalate to FFS review
