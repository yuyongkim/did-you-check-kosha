# Steel Factor Taxonomy
Status: Draft

## Main Factor Groups
- Structural basis: member type, grade, span, restraint condition
- Loading: dead, live, piping, tray, equipment, wind or seismic screening
- Degradation: corrosion loss, section reduction, connection distress
- Serviceability: deflection, vibration, alignment with supported systems
- Fabrication and repair: access, weldability, reinforcement strategy

## Governing Questions
- What drives member or connection governing ratio?
- When does serviceability control over strength?
- Which loads originate from other disciplines?
