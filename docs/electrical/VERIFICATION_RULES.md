# Electrical Verification Rules
Status: Draft

## Layer Checks
- Layer 1: electrical calculation and unit consistency
- Layer 2: protection and safety consensus
- Layer 3: fault, arc-flash, and PQ red-flag screening
- Layer 4: reverse coordination and scenario checks

## Core Red Flags
- Fault current exceeds interrupting rating
- Arc-flash energy exceeds workability limit
- Protection settings inconsistent with system duty
- Harmonics or voltage drop exceed allowable margin
- Transformer health indicates unstable service risk
