# Civil Factor Taxonomy
Status: Draft

## Main Factor Groups
- Structural basis: member type, reinforcement, load path, support condition
- Durability: cover, exposure class, carbonation, corrosion initiation risk
- Distress state: crack width, spalling, settlement, substantial damage
- Interface condition: anchor bolts, base plates, attached equipment
- Repair context: accessibility, outage timing, monitoring needs

## Governing Questions
- What determines structural adequacy versus serviceability concern?
- What damage mode controls urgency?
- Which issues propagate into rotating, steel, or piping systems?
